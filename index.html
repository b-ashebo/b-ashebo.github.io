Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Daniel K. Molzahn. I received my Master's degree in Data Science from the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) as a Mastercard Foundation Scholar, and my B.Eng. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Wachemo University.

My research focuses on optimization, reinforcement learning, and machine learning for large-scale cyber-physical systems, with applications in power grid security, cyberattack modeling, and sequential decision-making using multi-armed bandit methods.
